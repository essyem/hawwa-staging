{% trans "Postpartum Nutrition Tips" %}

{% trans "Simple tips to enhance your nutritional intake during the postpartum period" %}

{% trans "Eat Regularly" %}

{% trans "Aim to eat small, frequent meals every 2-3 hours to maintain energy levels and support milk production. Keep healthy snacks within reach, especially during feeding sessions." %}

{% trans "Stay Hydrated" %}

{% trans "Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Keep a water bottle nearby during breastfeeding sessions. Herbal teas like fenugreek and fennel can support lactation." %}

{% trans "Balance Nutrients" %}

{% trans "Include protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates in each meal. Focus on iron-rich foods to replenish blood loss, and calcium-rich foods for bone health and milk production." %}

{% trans "Omega-3 Fatty Acids" %}

{% trans "Include sources of omega-3s like fatty fish, chia seeds, and walnuts to support brain development for your baby and reduce inflammation in your body." %}

{% trans "Fiber-Rich Foods" %}

{% trans "Gradually increase fiber intake with fruits, vegetables, and whole grains to prevent constipation and support digestive health during recovery." %}

{% trans "Lactation-Supporting Foods" %}

{% trans "Incorporate galactagogues like oats, fenugreek, fennel, and chickpeas that may help increase milk production for breastfeeding mothers." %}
